Facility

Within all of our campus, we ensure that our students are comforted with numerous amount of spaces availabvble all across our campuses to relax and to also be productive. We have multiple cafe in our main campus including vending machines around to ensure that our students are able to purchase snacks, as we do consider about cost of living and student expenses.

There are countless amount of study areas for our students where they can do individual and group work across all of our campuses, to ensure that our students are able to get the best out of the space available.

Our library services are available within all of our campuses to ensure that our students at City hgain the support and resources they need for their studies. We ensured that our libraries have resources for every topic and modules touched upon our multiple courses within our University. Our libraries also have printing and computer services for our students to utilise for their own need. We make sure that we have everything for students to be successful.
